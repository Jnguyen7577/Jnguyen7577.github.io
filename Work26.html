Description

"Tatsuo on the Beach" is a side-scroller where you play as Tatsuo as he surfs his way to the finish while jumping waves and dodging obstacles such as pigs and rocks. This project was made by a team of 3, one programmer and two artists.

I worked on the character design and animation and coordinated with the programmer about the game mechanics and level design.
