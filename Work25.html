Description

Tatsuo is a character I created for various projects including "Tatsuo on the Beach" and "Tatsuo"(game). This is a 3d model of him which will be used in a "create your adventure" genre game. Initially Tatsuo had dragon-like textures but to maximize his cuteness he retains the smoother and simple appearance.
