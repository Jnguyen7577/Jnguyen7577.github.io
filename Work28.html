Maya and Premiere Pro: Stranger

Home

Dimensions: 1280 x 960


Description

In this short, the figure is seen missing their face to then find it suspended in air. It travels farther as the figure makes an attempt to obtain what is lost.

The challenge with this animation was animating the figure's gestures and having to portray some kind of struggle without giving it a voice. The animation was then taken to Premiere Pro where I would include royalty free audio that I edited using Audacity.
