About Me

I am Jason Nguyen, currently a digital media art major in the San Jose State University BFA Program. I enjoy working with various media such as Maya, Unity, and Aftereffects as well as refining my understanding with html, css, js coding and expanding more on 3D. I am always excited and open to learning more to add to my skill set. As of now my works revolve around the exploration of myself and who I am as an individual. I also enjoy putting the audience in unique experiences through works of art by triggering their emotions through visuals that I create.

My love for the arts and working with 3D comes from my love for video games and world building. When I am not working in virtual space I am choreographing and teaching dance where I can bring storytelling to physical space.
