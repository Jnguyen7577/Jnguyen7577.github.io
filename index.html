About Me

I am Jason Nguyen, a student majoring in digital media art at San Jose State University. I am currently developing my skills in various fields such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Aftereffects, html coding, and other mediums. Other forms of art I take part in is choreographing and teaching dance, I am currently training with Academy of Villains and teaching at Purdance. Once in a while I like to put together a crew and work on video projects whether it be music videos or dance videos. With these videos I get to combine the two things I love which is art and dance and explore the many possibilities of bringing both worlds together. When I am not doing art I enjoy playing video games, watching anime, and drinking tea.
